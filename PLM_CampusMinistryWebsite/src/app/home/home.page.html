Yes. A Collaborator is an employee of PLM. Under the guidance and supervision of the Campus Minister, they are tasked with aiding in the building of a faith-community on campus and assisting in campus ministry programs.

They are commissioned by the Campus Ministry Office and the Archdiocese of Manila to serve as a catalyst within his/her school community, ensuring the fulfillment of the Vision, Mission, and Goals of the Campus Ministry, so that presence of God may be magnified.
